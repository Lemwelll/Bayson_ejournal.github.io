Welcome to the official e-journal of the ELEC014 Educational / Industry Tour, conducted from January to April. This immersive industry experience took students across key in different technology hubs in the Philippines, providing invaluable insights into the professional world of computing and technology.

Tour Objectives

  • Bridge the gap between academic learning and industry applications
  • Expose students to current technologies and methodologies used in the industry
  • Provide networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Inspire career paths and specializations in various computing fields
  • Enhance understanding of workplace dynamics and professional expectations

This tour is a crucial component of the ELEC014 course, designed to complement theoretical learning with practical industry exposure, helping students make informed decisions about their future careers in technology.

PTV Davao Media Hub - People's Television Network (PTV)

The PTV Media Hub, specifically the Mindanao Media Hub, is a facility utilized by the People's Television Network (PTV), a government-owned media organization in the Philippines. It serves as a broadcasting hub for PTV and potentially other attached organizations of the Presidential Communications Office in the Mindanao region. DXNP-TV, channel 11, is a television station in Metro Davao and acts as the Mindanao flagship for PTV.

Key Technologies Observed:

  • Broadcast Automation Systems
  • IP-Based Broadcasting Infrastructure
  • Satellite and Terrestrial Signal Transmission
  • Cloud-Based Newsroom Systems
